Hi, I'm Henry. Thanks for checking out my site.

Web development is the most interesting type of programming for me because it is the fastest way to write software that can be available to anyone.

Unlike other software, web platforms can access anything available on the internet. In fact web platforms can actively affect the world for us based on our desires.

The right web platform could do almost anything for you that you could do online yourself. It could connect you with information from a top expert anywhere in the world, or help you meet someone who will change your life.

There are so many smart things that technology can do for us. A personal computer is almost like having a second brain that's thinking for us. I want to make this second brain as smart as possible.

This is why I've studied several aspects of web developement from hosted databases to front-end. I've created and consumed my own API. I'm a versatile programmer of serveral Python, Ruby and JavaScript frameworks.
